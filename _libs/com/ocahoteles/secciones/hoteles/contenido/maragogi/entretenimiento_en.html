Without any kind of doubt our experienced leisure and entertainment team at Grand Oca Maragogi Beach & Leisure Resort is one of our particular strengths; they organize activities throughout the whole day with varied options for children and adults of all ages. Have fun and enjoy excellent day and night activities at our resort!


Children entertainment


Our leisure and entertainment team will show you different activities for children older than 4 years old, with specialized equipment and offering specific options for each age. At our Kids Club we offer indoor activities, as well as both swimming pool and beach activities. 


Opening Hours: 9:00 until 12:00 and 14:00 until 18:00

Age: from 4 years old up to 12 years old


Adult entertainment


Our experienced leisure and entertainment team will be in charge of managing our varied offer of leisure, available to suit all tastes and ages: there are sports, aquatic, recreational and dance activities, as well as having night shows and music at the Taba Disco. 

Please, consult our daily program. 


Opening hours: 9:00 until 02:00


Shows

The Theatre at the Esplanada tropical is where the performance of our shows takes place. There are varied shows with regional, national and international themes for adults and children that bring joy and entertainment to the night at the Grand Oca Maragogi Beach & Leisure Resort.
