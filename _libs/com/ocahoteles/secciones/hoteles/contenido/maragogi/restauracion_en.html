At Grand Oca Maragogi Beach & Leisure Resort we offer our clients an extensive range of cuisine, from the very traditional brazilian cuisine to the international cuisine, and also fusion cuisine. In our restaurant area you will find exclusive menus, Premium Bar service, cocktails…, always surrounded by a calm and pleasant atmosphere.

Dos Corais Restaurant

Dos Corais Restaurant has a capacity up to 340 people and is located opposite the sea. The restaurant has panorama windows creating, thus, a spacious effect. Breakfast is served from 07:00 until 10:00 and it has more than 70 different options, inspired by the regional cuisine. Regional sweets, great range of sweet and normal breads, bolo do goma, the well-known tapiocas with the most delicious fillings, cereals and yogurts. The buffet lunch is served from 12:00 until 15:00, here you can find salads, hot meals, pasta, garnishing and desserts. Show cooking. 

Ix’U Restaurant 

Close to the most beautiful beach in Maragogi, the new Ix’U Restaurant offers our clients a big variety of fusion cuisine in a “lounge” atmosphere. The contrast between the old and new, the exchange of experiences between cultures, and the diversity of techniques are mixed with our culture to create a unique and different gastronomic experience. It is worth mention our exclusive wine list, all of them are a special selection.

It is not part of the All inclusive option, it is mandatory to make the reservation at the Guest Service.

Capacity: up to 70 people.

Il Giardino Restaurant

It is a special italian cuisine buffet.

It is not part of the All inclusive option; R$50,000 per person shall be charged, no beverages included.

Coqueirais Bar

It is the main restaurant at our Resort, it has a panoramic terrace with views to the Ponta de Mangue Beach. It is located by the main swimming pool and it opens from 10:00 until 22:00 with drinks and snack service.

Galés Bar

Ponta de Mangue Bar

It is opposite Maragogi sea in the private beach of the Resort and it has astonishing views. It opens from 10:00 until 17:00 with snacks and beverage service.

Tropical Bar

It is located in the Espacio Esplanada Tropical, this place is perfect to enjoy it with the family. Enjoy the evening and taste the appetizers while watching the exclusive shows organized by our entertainment team. It opens from 21:00 until 23:00 with beverage service.

Terraço Miramar

It is a big terrace with sea-views, close to the Dos Corais Restaurant. It has tea service on the afternoons, from 16:00 until 17:30. During the entire day it is used as part of the restaurant and Tropical terrace.
