Halls for events



The event halls of Oca Villa de Avilés are characterized by their luminosity, excellent service and versatility. It has three modular rooms that can be used individually or together, becoming a great space for larger celebrations.

They are located on the main floor of the hotel and have large windows that overlook a quiet pedestrian street, from which a great source of natural light arrives.

All the rooms are equipped with the necessary audiovisual media so that the organization of business meetings, conventions and conferences becomes a success.

Its location is another of its great attractions, in the heart of the city and a few minutes from the Oscar Niemeyer International Cultural Center, a perfect location for all kinds of events.

In addition, the Island of Innovation is establishing itself as the new center of cultural and business activity of Avilés, placing our rooms as a point of reference and perfect complement for the development of any event.
