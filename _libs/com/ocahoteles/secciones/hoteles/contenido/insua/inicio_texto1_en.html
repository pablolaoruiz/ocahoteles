Oca Insua Hotel is located on the centre Costa da Morte (A Coruña), one step away from Ézaro, Muxía, Corcubión…, and in the middle of the Camino de Santiago to Finisterre, making, thus, Oca Insua Hotel a  compulsory pilgrim stop during their travel towards the end of the Camino. 

The town of Cee in the biggest city center in Costa da Morte. Its commercial area, multiscreen cinemas, leisure area, sundays market, etc…, provide to travelers varied leisure activities during their stay in Costa da Morte, making Oca Insua Hotel the best choice.


Accomodation

Oca Insua Hotel has 48 rooms divided in three floors, recently renovated to offer our guests the best accomodation to enjoy their stay at Costa da Morte. 

All rooms have:


• Private bathroom

• Telephone

• Hair dryer 

• Air conditioning system

• Heating system

• LED tv

• Free WiFi


Oca Insua Hotel also has family rooms, a suite with Jacuzzi and rooms specially adapted for guests with reduced mobility.
