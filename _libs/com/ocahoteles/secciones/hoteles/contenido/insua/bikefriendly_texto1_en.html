Hotel Oca Insua a Bike Friendly hotel to enjoy the Costa da Morte with your bike

Few lucky people have traveled the "end of the world" by bicycle, an experience with which to enjoy beautiful landscapes unique in Spain and Galicia.

If you like long bike rides and mountain routes, Costa Da Morte is an ideal destination to enjoy this practice.

Immerse yourself in the wild nature and discover its spectacular coastal landscapes. In Oca Insua Da Morte we have all the amenities for mountain biking and bike routes in the area and in Galicia, and we have the Bikefriendly seal.
