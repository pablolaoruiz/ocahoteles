Galicia is the ultimate destination for trekking lovers and Costa da Morte, where Finisterre is located, is a trekking destination at its finest. During the last years, the number of pilgrims who took the route Camino de Santiago - Finisterre - Muxía has never stopped growing. 


The beautiful O camiño dos Faros route (200 km) links the towns of Maplica and Finisterre and increasingly attracts more and more visitors that come to enjoy the fascinating path by the sea. The charm of Monte Pinedo, Senda Verde de Carnota and the hundreds of routes across Costa da Morte are the best choices to close with a flourish a unique trekking experience in Galicia.
