A Unique Golf Course

Come to Augas Santas and enjoy our 18 holes, unique in the province of Lugo. Our 5.344 metres of course is divided into eighteen holes, of which three are par 5, ten are par 4 and five are par 3, making a total of par 70. 

The main characteristic is its almost entirely flat course, with well protected greens. Through the course, you will find three big lakes and a river that flows through the course bordering the holes, which emphasizes the prominence of water on the course.
