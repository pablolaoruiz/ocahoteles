Oca Augas Santas Balneario & Golf Resort is located in Patón, only 20 minutes away from Ourense, in the middle of the Ribeira Sacra. It is a modern 4 stars hotel with 105 rooms, a spa centre with medicinal mineral water, which gives the complex its name, and a 18 holes golf course.

Regarding our culinary service, we offer our customers a careful cafeteria and restaurant service to enjoy all kinds of galician products, from the various foods and excellent soups that the Ribeira Sacra provides us. 


Accommodation


The Hotel combines the modernity of its materials and design with respect towards the harmony of the setting. It has 105 rooms of which 97 are double rooms, 4 are family rooms, 1 is double OCA DELUXE, 2 are suites and 5 are adapted rooms. *All the rooms have full equipment: separate bathroom with washbasin, minibar, telephone, safe box, piped music, satellite television, air conditioning system, internet connection. 



Facilities

The hotel has spa center, golf course, business center, parking garage, medical service, fax, left-luggage office, cafeteria, social hall, landscaped gardens, access for disabled people, baggage porter, press, safe box.
