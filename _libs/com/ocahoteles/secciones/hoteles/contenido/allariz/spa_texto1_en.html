Oca Vila de Allariz Thermal Centre puts at our customers’ disposal a wide range of thermal treatments, in a quiet atmosphere which invites you to get deeply relaxed, turning this temple dedicated to relax into a perfect place to disconnect from daily stress and visit the province of Ourense or even all Galicia.

The individual zone has massage rooms, thermal jet, Niagara bath, mud and seaweed wraps, facial treatments, body treatments and rest area.

The collective zone offers a complete thermal-recreational circuit that has a dynamic swimming pool, steam bath, dry sauna, ice room, peeling shower and Tropical shower. The dynamic swimming pool is equipped with a four meter waterfall which falls from the upper floor, waterbeds, stream water, swan neck jets, hydromassage jets set at different heights, bubble seats and hydromassage seats. 

 The natural environment by the Arnoia river puts the finishing touch to the full relax experience offered by our Thermal Centre in Allariz.
