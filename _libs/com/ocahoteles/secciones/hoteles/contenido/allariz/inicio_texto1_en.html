Vila de Allariz Hotel is located on one of the most beautiful towns in Ourense -and even in all Galicia: the town of Allariz, by the Arnoia river and 10 minutes away from city centre of Ourense by car. 

The beautiful views, its closeness to nature and the quiet pace of life in Allariz, make of Vila de Allariz Hotel the ideal place to enjoy a quiet and relaxed stay during your visit. 


Accommodation

The hotel has 38 rooms decorated with contemporary style and a suite with certain comforts such as high-speed internet with cost (WiFi is free in common areas), air conditioning system, telephone, ironing and board ironing, hair dryer, 24 hour room service, minibar in all rooms and flat screen Tv. This hotel is 100% smoke-free. 


Facilities

The facilities available in Vila de Allariz Hotel include:


• Restaurant

• Gym

• Cafeteria

• Spa

• Café

• Wide gardens

• Event hall with capacity up to 480 guests, perfect to celebrate weddings and conferences
