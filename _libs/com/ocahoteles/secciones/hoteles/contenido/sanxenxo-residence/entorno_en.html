Our residence is located close to the towns in the Rías Baixas, where there are a lot of beaches, esplanades, fishing harbours… In the surrounding area you can go on walking trips visiting historic monuments, county houses, churches, archaeological remains, winery cellars…

The closest urban areas like Sanxenxo, Portonovo and O Grove offer a great variety of leisure pubs, both for young people and for people who want to spend time with their families.

Sports

If you like to practice sports during holidays…, horse riding, go karts, quads, diving, fishing, hiking, paintball, parascending, tennis, paddle, golf, swimming, surf, sailing, bodyboard, canoeing, mountain bikes…, these are only a few examples of the sports you can practice. It is worth mention the numerous hiking routes that go through natural and rural areas, which can be done walking, by horse or bike riding.

Leisure

In Sanxenxo and Portonovo, as well as in the nearest urban areas like O Grove, Cambados and Pontevedra there are a lot of options both for the young ones and adults to have fun. Among these, it is worth to mention museums, visits to monuments, Spa centers, boat trips, cinemas, balls park, bowling alley, bingo, casino, alehouses, pubs, discos…

Cuisine

The Galician cuisine is characterised by the use of land and sea products and a careful preparation that always delights everyone who tastes it. In Sanxenxo, Portonovo and the nearest fishing towns you will find a big range of restaurants, taperías and rotisseries, where you can taste Galician delicacies. Besides, during summer there are a lot of gastronomic feasts where you can savour seafood. 

What to visit

Santiago de Compostela

One of the most important World Heritage Sites without any kind of doubt

Monuments area of Pontevedra

Astonishing urban areas which combine history, culture, leisure, and landscape with that special

touch of charm thanks to its antiquity.

Vigo

Discover its historic quarter: a small town in the middle of an industrial and bussy city

Islas Atlánticas National Park

It comprises the Cíes Islands, and the Isles of Ons, Cortegada and Sálvora; it has cliffs on the west side and breathtaking beaches and dunes on the east side. The isles have the most important and spectacular marine birds colonies in the entire Spanish coast as well as extremely rich bottom sea. 

Rías Baixas

Ribeira, O Grove, Cambados, Illa de Arousa, Combarro, Marín, Cangas de Morrazo, Baiona, Tui…, these are the fishing towns par excellence in the Rías Baixas of Galicia.
