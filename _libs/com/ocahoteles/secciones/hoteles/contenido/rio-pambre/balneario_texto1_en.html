Our Spa Center has various treatments to relieve stress and take you into a state of full relaxation. We have bubble and hydromassage baths, thermal jet, inhalations, mud and algae wrap treatments, facial treatments, and different kinds of massages. In the massages, we must point out the Celtic massage, a combination of warm stones and essential oils which create a calm and quiet atmosphere and connects us to our ancestors. 

The collective area has a swimming pool, steam bath, sauna, peeling showers and cold pools with mineral waters of medicinal use. The full spa experience is completed by water beds, hydromassage seats, swan neck jets and waterfalls at 34ºC. Thanks to this circuit you will be able to disconnect from everyday stress.

There is an adjacent rest area where you can get even more relaxed while having an infusion at the end of your treatments.
