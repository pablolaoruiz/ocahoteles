45 minutes away from Santiago, 20 minutes away from Lugo and 50 minutes away from A Coruña, in the middle of the Ulloa region, you can find our cosy Spa, in a 5 hectares area crossed by the Pambre River and surrounded by native forests with centenary trees, very close to the Castle of Pambre. Bathed by the crystal clear and lively waters of the Pambre River, our spa was built following bioclimatic criteria, using characteristic materials from the Galician architecture such as stones, wood and slate. It is a temple of relax and enjoyment of nature. 


Rooms

We have 23 rooms, 11 of them in the main building and 12 in stone pallozas with thatched roof, as it is traditional of this area. All rooms have bathroom, telephone, safe box, air conditioning system, heating system, television and WiFi.

The pallozas are divided into two parts, each of them having three bedrooms and a living room. It is perfect for groups of friends who want to get relaxed in Galicia and who want a private and intimate atmosphere. 


Facilities

We also have a restaurant, bar-cafeteria and, of course, our fantastic Spa of natural water.
