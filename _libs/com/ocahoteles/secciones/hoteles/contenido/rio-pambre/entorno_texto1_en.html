We are only 2,8 km away from the Camino de Santiago (Casanova), in the middle of the route that crosses the center of Galicia, in the municipality of Palas de Rei (Lugo), 60 km away from Santiago de Compostela, and 45 km away from Lugo. Ourense, A Coruña and Pontevedra are less than 100 km away. 

We are at the center of the Ulloa region, very well known for its country houses and for the first pilgrims hospital along the Camino in Galicia. Our Spa is located on an unparalleled area where you can enjoy Galicia at its finest.

To the east, you will find the Castle of Pambre, a Medieval monument, that provides a characteristic aura to the landscape and fills the air with old legends, along with the remains of a Celtic hill fort and the old Camino Real, which connected Ourense and Santiago. The Pambre River joins with the Ulla River where the picturesque Torrentes do Macara end (only the most experienced and highly qualified canoeists dare to descend it). 

Eagles, roe deers, wild boars, trouts or even otters are part of our neighbourhood and sometimes even wolves come by.
