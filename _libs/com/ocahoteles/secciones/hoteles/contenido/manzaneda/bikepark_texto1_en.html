The bike park at the Manzaneda Ski and Mountain Resort is a treat for mountain bike enthusiasts. 

Manzaneda has one of the best Bike Parks in Spain. Six tracks, wooden platforms, jumps and paths designed to all kinds of levels, offering high levels of security and fun to bike enthusiasts, with the help of the 6 spots-chair lift and special events during the season. It is perfect for Downhill lovers.

Bike Park Services:

• Bike Patrols

Our team of Bike Patrols ensures for your security and the maintenance of the tracks every weekend. Besides, you can also find out about the resort, tracks and the best parts of our bike park. 

• Bike Wash Station

Bike Park Manzaneda puts at your disposal a wash station so you can carefully clean you bike at the end of your day. You will find our station near the lockers.
