Weddings and Events

Oca Puerta del Camino is located on an extraordinary area to the north of Santiago de Compostela, in the residential area of San Lázaro. It is surrounded by vast green areas and it has good transport accessibility. Among our facilities, it is should be pointed out our jardín inglés with direct entrance to the banquet halls. Our open spaces and our long experience in the organization of events as well as an impeccable cuisine make Oca Puerta del Camino the ideal place for any kind of social celebration. 

Customized Service

Our team, experts in organizing social events and at customer orientation, is at your disposal to guide you in the organization of your event. We will make sure all your suggestions and preferences are taken into account to create a unique and a-once-in-a-lifetime event.

Cuisine

Pablo Vila leads the cuisine at Oca Puerta del Camino, while Daniel Rey is the maître d’hôtel. This professional duo mix top-quality cuisine and fresh products with a painstaking service, but adapting the dishes to all suggestions given by the organizer, so everything is perfect for your event. 

Rooms and capacities

Oca Puerta del Camino has highly versatile halls; we can have groups form 10 up to 550 guests, and even 1.200 if it is a cocktail event. All the rooms are elegantly and warmly decorated. The halls have natural light and garden-views, and it is possible to customize their decoration for your event. The outdoor swimming pool and the garden or the Piano Bar are charming locations where you can organize the welcome cocktail. 

Conferences and Conventions

Oca Puerta del Camino Hotel has elegant and spacious meeting and events rooms located in Santiago de Compostela, attached to the House of Congress of Galicia. You will be able to choose between its 1.500 m2 of rooms, most of them with natural light. Oca Puerta del Camino Hotel is a hotel specialised in large conferences, conventions and small meetings, so we can provide you a comprehensive solution for organizing your event. Both the hotel and the House of Congress attached are an exemplary model of the organization of events in Galicia thanks to its perfect location, accessibility, large capacity, great parking space and fantastic human team. Our customized service and our pursuit of excellence make our professional team the best guarantee of success for your event:

 

• In the northern area of Santiago de Compostela, 2 minutes away from AP-9 connection. 

• 5 minutes away by car from the Cathedral of Santiago

• 10 minutes away by car from the Airport

• Attached to the House of Congress and Exhibition Centre.
