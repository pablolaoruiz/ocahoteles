Berenguela Restaurant

Berenguela Restaurant offers a cuisine based on fresh and season product, always bearing in mind the style of the Galician cuisine. Taste our menu prepared with Galician products, combined with various Designation Origin soups from Galicia and Spain. 

The restaurant has 5 private halls with capacity from 2 up to 50 people. Both the Berenguela Restaurant and some of its private halls can be booked to celebrate a private event. 


Golden Goose Gastrobar

Recently opened, Golden Goose Gastrobar offers another type of cuisine, a more casual one, to our customers. It has a cafeteria area with bar and two separate halls to relax and enjoy our selection of tapas and cocktails in a cosmopolitan environment.

Our piano bar hall is perfect for your meeting or for having drinks or coffee.
