Oca Puerta del Camino, a four stars hotel and part of the Oca Hotels chain, offers a modern luxury in a traditional space. It is located opposite the House of Congress (the business area of the city), 15 minutes away from Lavacolla airport (by the AP-9) and only 3,5 km walk away from the Cathedral of Santiago. It has an excellent communication to the main business and shopping centers of the area. Its spacious and elegant facilities, along with its excellent cuisine, make it an example of meeting point and business location in Santiago de Compostela. 


Accomodation

In all our 160 rooms we combine fine materials with classic decoration, spacious areas, and natural light. The hotel has 152 double rooms, 4 of them adapted for disabled guests, and 8 of them are suites. All rooms have full equipped bathroom, minibar, telephone, hairdryer, toiletries, safe box, LED television with international channels, and free WiFi. 


Standard Room


Spacious rooms with big windows that let natural light in into the 30m2 room. It has a big bathroom with telephone, hairdryer, full-length mirror, and exclusive toiletries. It has free WiFi, LED television, safe box service, minibar and room service. If the customer needs it, they can ask for an extra bed either for adults or children (under availability). There are special rooms for smokers and it can be possible for customers to stay here with their pets (under availability). 


Junior Suite


Spacious 60 m2 rooms with hall, bedroom and living room where you can have meals. For our business customers we have available a desk, perfect to make any kind of project. This room has air-conditioning, free WiFi, safe box, 2 LED televisions with video on demand, fax service, pillow menu, and the possibility of having an extra bed for adult or children on demand and under availability. Our customers have the chance to relax in a hydromassage bath while they enjoy a cup of tea, a drink or appetizers. The bathroom has exclusive toiletries, full-length mirror, hairdryer and mirror for those who want to apply makeup. Room service is available from 8:00 until 23:00 with a special menu depending on the opening hours of the restaurant. 


Adapted rooms


We have 4 rooms for disabled guests which have all services needed. They are spacious rooms with big windows that let the natural light in into the 30m2 room. It has free WiFi, LED television, safe box service, minibar and room service. If the customer needs it, they can ask for an extra bed either for adults or children (under availability). There are special rooms for smokers and we have 2 adapted rooms where dogs can also stay (under availability, extra cost 20€ per day).

Facilities

The hotel has business center, parking lot, fax service, cloakroom, café, outdoor swimming pool, special facilities for disabled customers, baggage porter, press and safe box. 


Cuisine

Oca Puerta del Camino Hotel has the following services:


• Berenguela Restaurant: traditional cuisine in a vast area and 5 private dining rooms to celebrate all kinds of events.

• Golden Goose Gastrobar: located in hall of the hotel, it has a fast food menu as well as a extensive menu of combination plates. 

• Halls: Oca Puerta del Camino Hotel has various halls with natural light where you can celebrate any kind of event.
