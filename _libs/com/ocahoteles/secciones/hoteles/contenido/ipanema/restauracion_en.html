We have a varied gastronomic offer, based on traditional galician cuisine and made with first quality regional products. Our fresh ingredients give that extra quality that our customer can taste in the flavor of our dishes. Apart from the menu of our restaurant, we also have an everyday menu with different choices and a special company menu.


Opening hours:

Restaurant opens from Mondays to Thursdays from 20:00 until 23:00.

Cafeteria opens from Mondays to Fridays from 07:00 until 23:00 and during weekends from 08:00 until 14:00.


Cafeteria:

We have a menu of sandwiches, combination plates and pies to eat at any time, during the cafeteria’s opening hours. We also have a wide pavement café where you can enjoy the wonderful climate of Vigo and its astonishing views.
