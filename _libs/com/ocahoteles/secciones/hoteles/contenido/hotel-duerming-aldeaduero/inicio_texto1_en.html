Aldeaduero Apartments are surrounded by an unparalleled environment; this old town was restored to transform it into one of the prime examples of rural tourism in Spain. 

Thanks to its restoration, it has all the comfort to enjoy the rest and freedom you need. 

Three of its rooms have a wonderful terrace where you can take delight in the marvelous view over the Duero River.

We offer you several types of accommodation, the best gastronomy in the area, outdoor sport, large space for celebration, meeting rooms, and one step away from Portugal.
