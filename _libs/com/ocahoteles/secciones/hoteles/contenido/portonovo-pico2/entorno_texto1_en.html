EXCEPTIONAL LOCATION

The fishing town of Portonovo is located in the estuary of Pontevedra and it is one of the most visited touristic places. Travelers who arrive to this area are looking for beaches and tranquility to spend a couple of relaxing days.

During the day there is a calm atmosphere in the town, and it is not very crowded, so everyone can peacefully enjoy the beaches. At night, the atmosphere lights up thanks to the various pubs and night clubs.


BALTAR BEACH

 This beautiful beach is located at the center of Portonovo. It has white and fine sand, calm waters and only from time to time a tranquil wave comes to visit. It is surrounded by dunes, which are the ones that separate the beach from the road.

The beach is 600 meters long approximately and it has various services, such as parking lot, showers, bathrooms, and lifeguard stands, among others. There are many entrances to the beach through wooden paths that will lead you to ramps or natural paths down to the beach. Here you can practice water sports. This area is delimited by the Portonovo marina. 

It usually is kind of crowded, especially during summer because of the high amount of visitors in this area. Although it is an urban beach, it is very nice, cosy and it is worth visiting. 


CANELIÑAS BEACH

More than a beach, it is a breath-taking cove with transparent waters and with a beautiful green emerald color, with white, fine sand and very flat. Caneliñas is a calm and urban beach, surrounded by hotels and a few restaurants, and quite close to the center of Portonovo and the historic center. 


CANELAS, PAXARIÑAS, MONTALVO, AND MAJOR BEACHES

If you prefer a bigger beach with swell, when going out of Portonovo, you can find Canelas Beach and a little bit forward in the coast road, you will find the beautiful Paxariñas beach. We cannot forget about the big sandy ground of Montalvo and about Major Beach, each more beautiful than the preceding ones.


SAILING CLUB

The sailing club of Portonovo is located by the most touristic town during summer time: Sanxenxo, in the middle of O Salnés a comarca in Pontevedra.

It has not been long since the harbour area was humanized, turning the walk into a place for leisure and relaxation, with green areas, wooden floors and benches. At the end of this promenade you can find the building of the sailing club. 

The Sailing Club of Portonovo was founded in August in 1992 and its main activities are related to water sports, specially sailing, fishing or diving, all of them have their own sports sections. At the beginning of the year 2002 a section of underwater activities was created; here they teach diving lesson and have all equipment needed to participate in this activity, like bottles, autonomous equipment…


LA LONJA

At the end of the promenade, by the harbour and in the same roundabout where you can see the famous sculpture of Portonovo, you will find “La Lonja del pescado” (a fish market). Although it may seem weird, it is one of the most visited and recommended touristic places in this area of the Galician coast.

It is one of the main economic source of the town. It is made by three buildings where workers prepare the fishing equipment. 

The harbour has gone under several alteration because of the heavy storms that sometimes reach this coast. It also has a cafeteria and a special area to sell fish to traders and individuals.



A PEIXEIRA SQUARE

In the small square you can find the viewpoint that shares the same name; these sculptures represent the main profession that has supported the people of this area years after years: fishing. They represent two key elements and the most significant ones: an anchor, and the figure of a woman with a basket full of fish on her head. They are made in bronze and located in a small garden surrounded by benches so people can rest while admiring them.


OUTDOOR SPORTS

If you enjoy practicing sports while being on holidays, in the vicinity of Portonovo you have loads of choices: horse riding, kart racing, quads, scuba diving, paintball, golf, swimming, surf…, these are only a few examples. It is worth mentioning routes through natural and rural areas which can be made walking, horse riding or bike riding.


CUISINE

Galician cuisine includes both sea and land product with excellent preparation. In Sanxenxo, Portonovo, and the nearest fishing towns there are many restaurants, taperías and carveries where you can enjoy Galician delicacies. 

Besides, during summer there are a lot of gastronomic festivities, in which you can taste traditional sea products.
