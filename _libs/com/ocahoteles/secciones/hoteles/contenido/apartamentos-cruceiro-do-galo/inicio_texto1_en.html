Enjoy the historic quarter of Santiago de Compostela like you have never done before at our Cruceiro do Galo Apartments; they are very close to the Cathedral and to the historic quarter.

Our apartments are in a refurbished Galician emblematic building from the XIX century; this way every one of our visitors will find a place that cannot be matched since it combines tradition with nowadays modernity. You have excellent views of the Cathedral from the building; it is located only 8 minutes walk away. The surrounding is perfect for walking or riding a bike and enjoy the city. There is a parking lot close to our apartments.
