San Vicente Duerming Hotel** is located in the peninsule of O Grove, in a conservation forest, perfect to practice outdoor activities such as hiking or fishing. Our philosophy is to respect and preserve nature and because of that we fight to attain 100% of energetic efficiency with our solar panels and using the water from the natural spring. If what you seek is to connect with nature, San Vicente Duerming Hotel is the perfect match for you!

The hotel is on the outskirts of San Vicente de O Grove, 500 meters away from the sea, 5 km away from the well known A Lanzada Beach, 7 km away from O Grove and La Toja Island, and 15 km away from Sanxenxo. This perfect location will allow you to have a nice rest in a quiet and calm area while being in the perfect place in case you can to visit the Rías Bixas.
