What to see?

Beaches: only 5 minutes away from our hotel you can find more than 20 splendid beaches and coves, highlighting the 3 km of sandy area of the A Lanzada.


Aquarium: in the peninsula of O Grove you will find the first aquarium of Galicia where you will be able to see not only native species but species from all over the world. 


Museums: 10 minutes away from the hotel you will find the Ethnographic Museum of Fishing and Salting.


A Toxa: the A Toxa island, very well known for its medicinal thermal waters, belongs to the town hall of O Grove. Here you also have a casino, a chapel covered in scallop shells, and you can enjoy a very nice walk through its “central hill”, visit the 4 protected burro fariñeiro (only a few of these donkeys still exist), or have fun with your children at the children's park in the small village of the Grobits. 


Viewpoints: there are a lot of viewpoints through the region and in O Grove you can find the most astonishing two: one at Siradella hill and the other one in Con da Hedra.


Unique spots: the entire region is full of breathtaking sceneries, birds observatories, lighthouses, idyllic islands and small islands, old water mills, civil and religious architecture…



What to do?

Thanks to its perfect location you will be able to enjoy the beautiful coast and its dense interior vegetation. A few possible options are: hiking routes, thermalism, golf, horse riding, go karts, guided routes, and nightlife…,are a few options. Regarding water sports, you can practice diving, surf, kitesurf, windsurf…, and without having to leave O Grove.


To eat and enjoy eating:

We have the best seafood in the world, extremely fresh from the waters that you can see through our hotel’s windows. In O Grove, in October, we celebrate the Festa do Marisco, which attracts guests from all over the world. Something you cannot miss is to take a boat that will show you the Arousa estuary and that explains how bateas work (which are the breeding centers of mussels) or that can take you to Ons and Cíes Islands. Some of these boats have crystal bottoms so you can also see the sea bed. 


O salnés


Our hotel is located at the center of O Salnés region. At the heart of the Galician Rías Baixas is where you can find that region, which has countless resources, a vast range of activities and all you can wish to find. These are the perfect holidays for a demanding tourist. Its landscape and economy are defined by the geography, in which we must highlight two elements: on one side, the valley, surrounded by hills and crossed by the Umia River; on the other, the Arousa estuary, which provides a fishing atmosphere to the region. 


The name of the region itself derives from the fishing activity of the area, since it refers to the act of salting that was made here since ancient times; it was already mentioned in a document in the VI century. Nine of the town halls that form the region are: 

Cambados, O Grove, A Illa de Arousa, Meaño, Meis, Ribadumia, Sanxenxo, Vilagarcía de Arousa, and Vilanova de Arousa; thanks to this, we can have a fishing Salnés, a farming one, a rural or urban one, a cultural one, a touristic one, a fun-loving one, a calm one… To touch, see and feel is possible here thanks to its nature and beaches, its monuments, cuisine, facilities, history, leisure and sports activities, hospitality…


All this varied offer is at your disposal in a matter of minutes. You can go from the mountain to the coast and its beaches in the blink of an eye. You can become filled with its culture thanks to the monuments, writers and artists and then, at night, you can go out and have another kind of fun. This is not a place only for Summer; thanks to its mild and humid oceanic climate, we do not have extreme temperatures during winter (minimum temperature of 10ºC between December and February) nor during Summer (maximum temperature of 30ºC during July and August), which makes it the perfect place throughout the year. Apart from its coast and beaches, another interesting option is its cuisine, a extremely delicious one, which is at our disposal at any time.
