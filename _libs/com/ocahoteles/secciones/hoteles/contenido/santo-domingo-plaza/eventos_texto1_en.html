Oca Santo Domingo Plaza Hotel is strategically located at the entrance of Oviedo through the A-66 and only 5 minutes walk away from the historic district of the city. 


Built in a residential area, the hotel has excellent facilities; among them, we must highlight the indoor garden with direct access to the banquet halls. The spacious areas and a vast experience in organizing events, as well as a carefully prepared cuisine, make of  Oca Santo Domingo Plaza Hotel the ideal place for any kind of celebration. 


Customized Service

Our team, expert in the organization of social events and customer orientation, is at your disposal to guide you in the organization of your event so we make sure all your suggestions and preferences are taken into account in order to create a unique and a once-in-a-lifetime event. 


High quality Cuisine

Luis Alberto Martínez -chef and owner of Casa Fermín Restaurant, in Oviedo- directs the kitchen and the service at Oca Santo Domingo Plaza Hotel.


Luis Alberto combines high-quality cuisine and fresh products with a conscientious service, adapting to all suggestions made by the organizer so everything is perfect for your event. 


Halls and capacities

Oca Santo Domingo Plaza Hotel has highly versatile halls, with capacity from 10 up to 450 guests, reaching even 1.000 guests if it is a cocktail celebration. 


All our halls have an elegant and warm decoration which will guarantee the success of your event. Our halls have natural light and garden-views, and it is possible to match the decoration to your personal preference in order to customize your event 


The indoor Zen garden and the piano area are two charming locations where you can organize the welcome cocktail.
