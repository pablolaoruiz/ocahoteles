Oca Santo Domingo is a modern 4 stars hotel located on the centre of Oviedo, the capital of Asturias. We are only 5 minutes walk away from the historic district of the city and our hotel is easily accessible from the main access routes of the city. Our hotel has contemporary decoration and various multi-use halls with capacity to host any kind of events. We must point out our characteristic and relaxing Zen garden.



Rooms


We have 100 four-stars-quality rooms and stylish decoration, divided into three floors. 

Some of the services available in our rooms are: bathroom, minibar, telephone, hairdryer, toiletries, safe box, LED television, free WiFi, anti-fogging mirror, radiator towel rail or climate control system. 


Facilities

Among the spacious facilities of the Oca Santo Domingo Plaza you will find a business center, parking lot, gymnasium, conference hall, fax service, cloakroom, café, baggage porter, press and safe box.
