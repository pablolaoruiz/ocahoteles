Justo Hotel is a small and comfortable establishment located on an exceptional area in the most important Touristic Town in the Rías Baixas, Sanxenxo, along the beachfront of Silgar Beach and a few meters away from the Juan Carlos I marina.

It is a family oriented Hotel that follows a tradition that goes back to the year 1953  where everything began with a small guest house. 

Our hotel fits any kind of travelers, family, business, holidays or weekends; they all can benefit from our comfort and hospitality. 


Justo Hotel has 30 rooms, which can be single room, double room, double room with living room, and a mini suite, all of them fully equipped with bathroom, air conditioning system, heating system, piped music, television and telephone.
