Sanxenxo is one of the most touristic towns if the province of Pontevedra, and even the entire Galicia because, apart from having a great variety of resources, it is located on a exceptional place in the Galician geography. 


In Sanxenxo and its surrounding area, travelers can find everything: various beaches, the best cuisine with excellent wines and sea products, rural tourism in areas close to the town and great varieties of inns, cafeterias and pavement cafés in a relaxed atmosphere. 


You can also enjoy visiting near towns, such as Combarro, the fishing village of Portonovo of the luxurious Illa da Toxa.
