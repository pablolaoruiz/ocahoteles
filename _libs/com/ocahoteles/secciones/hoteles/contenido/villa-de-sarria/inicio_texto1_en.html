Accomodation

Our hotel has 28 bedrooms that were refurbished in the 2015, fully equipped with bathroom with hairdryer, satellite tv, heating and air conditioning system, telephone, piped music and safe box.


Facilities


Social living room, breakfast room* (with special service from 6:00 am so early birds pilgrims can start their last 100 km of the Camino). Garage service so you can leave your car while being on the Camino. Area for keeping bicycles.
