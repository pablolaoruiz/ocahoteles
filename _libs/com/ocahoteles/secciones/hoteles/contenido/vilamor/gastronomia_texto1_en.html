Gastronomy in Duerming Casa Vilamor.


In the dining room you can taste various dishes of regional food, of which many of its ingredients, complete their biological cycle within the house.

As for the wines, highlight the wines of variety Mencía and denomination of origin Ribeira Sacra, as well as Godellos and Albariños, already within the whites. All of them of a great quality for the enclave in which our establishment is located.

To finish any kind of food we recommend tasting some of our homemade liqueurs (herbs, coffee).
