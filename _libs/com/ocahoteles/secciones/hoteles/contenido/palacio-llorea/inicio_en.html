Oca Palacio de la Llorea Hotel**** is located only 7 km away from Gijón, the various beaches in the area, in La Llorea Golf Course, and well connected to the Autovía del Cantábrico (A-8). 

Oca Palacio de la Llorea Hotel was an old palace, now refurbished, that combines traditional architecture and avant-garde design, creating, thus, an unparalleled and unique atmosphere.

Accomodation

Our hotel has 62 modern rooms (2 of them Junior Suite) that have:


• Full equipped bathroom

• Hair dryer

• Heating system

• Air conditioning system

• Telephone

• Piped music

• Television

• Free WiFi connection


Our hotel has a restaurant, cafeteria, 4 multi-purpose halls for events and conferences up to 200 people, gymnasium and a Spa Centre. We also have an agreement with the 18 holes La Llorea Golf Course for the enjoyment of golf enthusiasts.



NOT WITHOUT YOU Program for Pets:

Oca Palacio de la Llorea is a hotel suited for dogs that do not weigh more than 15kg..

Please, remember to consult availability when doing the reservation; there are limited rooms for pets at the hotel.

These kinds of reservations have an extra cost of 15€.

At your arrival, you will be informed about the conditions of our pet friendly service at Oca Palacio de la Llorea.

Oca Palacio de la Llorea will provide you with a pet kit that will have:

- Feeder
- Drinker
- A toy for your pet (always new and with no sound)
- Cleaning kit with wipes, latex gloves, and hygiene bags
