Our agreement with La Llorea Golf Course provides our clients access to the 18 holes of this magnificent golf course. 

It is not a very long course and it is located nearly 100 meters above the sea level. 

Opening Hours

High season (1st of April - 30th of September):


• Opening time of the facilities: from 08:05 until 21:00*

• Beginning of golfing: from 8:05 until 20:30**

• Practice field: from 8:30 until 21:30


Low season (1st of October - 31st of March):


• Opening time of the facilities: from 08:05 until 20:00*

• Beginning of golfing: from 8:05 until 19:30**

• Practice field: from 8:30 until 20:30



*After the closing people are only allowed to access the lockers area until 21:45 during high season, and until 20:45 during low season.

**When light conditions allow it.
